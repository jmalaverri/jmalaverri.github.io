Joana Gonzales Malaverri

Postdoctoral Researcher

University of Campinas

I am a postdoctoral researcher at the Computer Research Laboratory (LRI) at University Paris Sud and also a member of the LaHDAK group. Former member of LIS Lab at IC-Unicamp, under the mentorship of Prof. Claudia Bauzer Medeiros. My research interests include, but are not limited to, scientific data management, data quality analysis, and software development. Nowadays, I am currently working on the development of a solution to assess the veracity of Knowledge Bases based on spatio-temporal context.
